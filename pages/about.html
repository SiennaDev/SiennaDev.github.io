ABOUT SIENNA

SIENNA's storage solutions are a great way to store your data, all type of files combined.
We're offering three differents plans to our customers, as well as a long-term, persistent, storage solution. The Bronze tier is limited to a maximum of 1Tb per subscription, it's the most economical plan for individuals. The Silver one is the most valuable for individual and small businesses, with a 2Tb limit, better data protection and higher data updates per week. The Gold tier is for medium and large businesses, with a 4Tb limit, the highest level of security whe can provide on site, and many advantages, such as more data updates and higher data recovery operations per week. You can also choose our Archive plan to store data for long-term conservation, in our vault, protected from any type of ingress.
Users can upload a defined amout of data per week, according to their subscription. If a user need to upload data and his upload limit is already reached for the active week, additional fees will be took into account. Data recovery can be done anytime, within the plan weekly limits. For higher data recovery operations, customers will have to contact the support team. Backups can be done on demand for additional costs.
Our data storage solutions are secured using a local & private network, as well as encryption methods and custom-made servers, running 24/7. For customers who subscribed to the Archive plan, custom storage solution can be used, to ensure a maximum protection against time, background radiations, dust and water, with military grade IP67 containers.
