ABOUT SIENNA

SIENNA's storage solutions are a great way to store your data, all type of files combined.
We're offering our users four data storage solutions, tailored to their needs and budget. Three of the plans we offer are for temporary data storage(monthly payment/annually renewable), while the last one offers a long-term storage solution on specialized hardware.The Bronze, Silver & Gold plans are designed for individuals and businesses who want a reliable, secure, enterprise-class data storage solution.The Archive plan offers a long-term storage solution on military-grade hardware, resistant to any type of ingress.
