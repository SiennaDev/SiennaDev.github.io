About SIENNA

SIENNA's storage servers are secured and ready to be fill with your data. You can access them using SSH or RDP, and store any type of file, such as photos, videos, documents, ... We're offering three differents plans, the Bronze tier is limited to a maximum of 1Tb per subscription, it's the most economical plan for individuals. The Silver one is the most valuable for individual and small businesses, with a 2Tb limit, a better data protection and a higher data updates amount per week. Finally, the Gold tier is for small to big businesses, with a 4Tb limit, the highest level of security whe can provide on site, and many advantages, such as more data updates and higher data recovery operations per week. You can also store data for long-term conservation in our vault, protected from any type of ingress.
