DATA RECOVERY SOLUTIONS

We're offering our customers data recovery solutions on different media types. You will have to send us your data storage medium, on which we will recover informations.For the data restoration, you can provide your own medium, or you can buy one from us, and we'll send it back to you, depending on the priority plan you'll choose.Currently, we can retrieve data from any type of hard-disk drive(SATA, IDE, SAS, SCSI), as well as modern solid-state drives(SATA 2.5", M.2 NVMe/SATA, PCIe, U.2 and SATA Express).For other devices (USB Keys, SD Card, Compact Flash cards, eUSB SSD, Floppy Disks and ZIP Disks) you'll have to choose the Starter plan.
