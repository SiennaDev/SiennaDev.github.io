STORAGE SOLUTIONS

SIENNA's storage servers are secured and ready to be fill with your data. You can access them using SSH or RDP, and store any type of file, such as photos, videos, documents, ... We're offering three differents plans, the Bronze tier is limited to a maximum of 1Tb per subscription, it's the most economical plan for individuals. The Silver one is the most valuable for individual and small businesses, with a 2Tb limit, a better data protection and a higher data updates amount per week. Finally, the Gold tier is for small to big businesses, with a 4Tb limit, the highest level of security whe can provide on site, and many advantages, such as more data updates and higher data recovery operations per week. You can also store data for long-term conservation in our vault, protected from any type of ingress.

DATA UPLOADS & RECOVERY

Users can upload a defined data amount per week to their storage solution, according to their subscription. If a user need to upload data and his upload limit is already reached for the active week, additional fees will be took into account. Data recovery can be done anytime, within the plan weekly limits. For higher data recovery operations, customers will have to contact the support. Backups can be done on demand for additional costs.

SECURITY & DATA PROTECTION

Our data storage solutions are secured using a local & private network, as well as encryption methods and custom-made servers, running 24/7. For customers who subscribed to the Archive plan, custom storage solution can be used, to ensure a maximum protection against time, background radiations, dust and water, with military grade IP67 containers.
